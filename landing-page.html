Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Bramp?

Sign up via the app or website, verify your account, and complete your KYC. Fund your wallet to start buying, selling, and trading crypto and gift cards. Our platform is user-friendly and support is available.

What cryptocurrencies can I buy and sell?

You can buy and sell USDT (ERC20 & TRC20), Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), and Tron (TRX). We plan to add more cryptocurrencies in the future. Stay tuned for updates!

How long do crypto transactions take?

Transactions can be completed in less than 2 minutes depending on the cryptocurrency network

Is my wallet secure?

Yes, your Bramp wallet is powered by a trusted infrastructure with robust security measures. We employ industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your funds and data.

Why do I need to complete KYC?

KYC (Know Your Customer) helps us ensure the security of our platform, prevent fraud, and comply with regulatory requirements in various regions. It helps us build a safer environment for all users.

What documents are required for KYC?

Typically, you'll need to provide a valid government-issued ID (like a passport, national ID card, or driver's license) and sometimes proof of address. The specific requirements will be clearly outlined during the KYC process.
